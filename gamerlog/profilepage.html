Uncharted 4: A thief's end (PS4)

Uncharted 4 is an absolute masterpiece. This is a game that hooked me from the beginning and didn't let go until the final frame.The main idea behind these kinds of games, I'm guessing, is to make it feel like you're watching a very good Hollywood adventure film, but additionally allowing the viewer to fully participate in the story. The thing about Uncharted 4 is that, it holds your attention so well that you can't just play it once for a little bit and stop for a few days. You have to complete this game once you start, and play it a little bit sequentially each day. Don't worry though. This is not a game that would overstay it's welcome for most people. It took me around 13 and 1/2 hours to beat it. [Oct 17, 2019]
